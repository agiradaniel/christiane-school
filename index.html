WHO WE ARE

Christiane school is a christian based mixed primary school founded in 2004 in kitengela town Noonkopir area, the school begun in a humble setting of only one classroom and one pupil and has since grown with an upward trajectory and now we have enough facilities,teachers and staff to accommodate over 700 pupils, over the years we have done a number of KCPE exams and performed tremendously putting our school on the radar in kitengela town and it’s environs, our school is founded on strong values and is aimed to produce all rounded students who will stand out as principled individuals and leaders in the society, we are still growing and plans to build a junior secondary are underway in compliance with the competence based curriculum system, we are looking forward to admit more students and grow the christiane fraternity.

WHY BRING YOUR CHILD TO CHRISTIANE

Christiane school provides a nurturing environment for your child to grow spiritually, discover their talents and also excel academically, we have professional teaching and non-teaching staff that provide the highest quality of service and have been trained to give each child respect and equal treatment, we also have enough facilities to accommodate every student and ensure that everyone has a comfortable learning environment, the school director oversees the operations of the school to ensure that the everything is running smoothly
