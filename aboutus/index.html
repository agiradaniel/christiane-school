Our Vision

Our Mission

At Christiane School, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing learning environment that fosters academic excellence, moral values, and personal growth. We strive to empower our students with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and a strong sense of social responsibility. Through a competent and caring faculty, and a commitment to continuous improvement, we aim to equip our students to excel academically and become compassionate leaders who positively contribute to their communities and the world at large.

WHO WE ARE

Christiane School, established in 2004 within Kitengela's Noonkopir region, is a Christian-based mixed primary institution. From its humble origins with just one classroom and a single pupil, the school has evolved significantly. Today, boasting a robust upward trajectory, we possess ample facilities, a dedicated teaching faculty, and a proficient support staff, capable of accommodating a student population exceeding 700. Throughout our journey, we've consistently excelled in KCPE exams, thus prominently positioning our institution in Kitengela town and its surroundings.

Built upon a bedrock of unwavering values, our school is committed to cultivating well-rounded individuals primed to shine as principled trailblazers in society. Our expansion continues, underscored by the recent introduction of a junior secondary segment aligned with the competence-based curriculum. With optimism, we anticipate further growth in both admissions and the Christiane community, continuing our journey of educational excellence
